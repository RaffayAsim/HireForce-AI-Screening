# Airtable Setup Guide

## Adding "AI" Department Option

To enable the "AI" department option in your job creation form, you need to add it to your Airtable table:

### Step 1: Open Your Airtable Base
1. Go to [Airtable](https://airtable.com)
2. Open your base: `appGUsrYT1rV9ZtCW`
3. Navigate to the **Jobs** table

### Step 2: Find the Department Field
1. Look for the column/field named "Department"
2. Click on the field header to open field settings

### Step 3: Add "AI" Option
1. In the field configuration, find the "Options" or "Select Options" section
2. Add "AI" to the list of available options
3. Save the field configuration

### Step 4: Verify
The dropdown in your app now includes "AI" as an option, and job creation will work seamlessly.

## Testing
After adding "AI" to your Airtable Department field:
1. Try creating a new job
2. Select "AI" from the Department dropdown
3. The job should create successfully without any errors

## Troubleshooting
If you still get the "Insufficient permissions" error:
1. Double-check that "AI" is exactly spelled the same in Airtable and the app
2. Make sure there are no extra spaces or special characters
3. Verify the field type is "Single Select" or "Multiple Select" (not text)
